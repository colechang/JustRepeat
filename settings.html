How to Use Your Extension

Once the extension is installed, you can use it to create and manage loops in YouTube videos. Here's how to use the extension:

  1. Open a YouTube video in your browser.
  2. Drag the range slider to select the end time of the loop.
  3. Click on the Loop button to create a loop at the youtube player current time.

  5. If the loop was unsuccessful
